What is Canine Distemper? +

Canine distemper is a highly contagious viral disease affecting dogs, especially puppies and unvaccinated animals. It impacts the respiratory, digestive, and nervous systems.

Common Symptoms +

Symptoms include fever, nasal discharge, coughing, vomiting, diarrhea, seizures, muscle twitching, and hardened paw pads.

Why Early Awareness Matters +

Early detection and vaccination greatly improve survival rates and prevent spread to other dogs in the community.
